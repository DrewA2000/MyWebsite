My Bio

Hi, I'm Drew Anastasio, a senior at Texas A&M University.. When it comes to accessibly in computing, I believe this is a topic that is not talked about enough, and I took this class in order to learn more about it. I had no idea that accessibly in computing had come as far as it has. Something that I am super interested in is screen readers. I recently watched a video on a blind person using the computer through screen readers and it was something I never even could have imagined. Before this class I just thought blind people couldn't use computers.

Here are a few of my interests in accessibility

  • Accessible design principles
  • Screen reader optimization
  • Keyboard navigation
  • Alt text for images
